COOKIE POLICY

Last Updated: January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them on Ludo MINIMA, and how you can manage your preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help the website function properly, analyze traffic, and serve relevant advertisements.

2. How We Use "Local Storage" [ Essential ]

Unlike traditional cookies, we primarily use a modern web technology called Local Storage to improve your gaming experience. This data remains on your device and is not sent to our servers.

  • Game Progress: We save your current game state so you don't lose progress if you refresh the page.
  • Settings: We remember your preferences, such as sound volume and theme choices.

3. Advertising Cookies [ Google AdSense ]

To keep this game free to play, we use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website.

  • Google DoubleClick DART Cookie: Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites on the Internet.
  • Personalization: These cookies help display ads that are relevant to your interests.

You may opt-out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Managing Cookies

You have the right to accept or refuse cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer.

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data.
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Block all cookies.
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.

Note: Blocking "Local Storage" or cookies may prevent the game from saving your progress.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at support@ludominima.com.
