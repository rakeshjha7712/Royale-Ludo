GAME RULES

Ludo is a classic strategy board game played between 2 to 4 players. While the game relies on the luck of the dice, understanding the rules and employing the right strategy is key to becoming the Ludo MINIMA champion.

1. The Objective

The main goal of the game is simple: be the first player to race all four of your tokens from your home base to the center of the board (the Home triangle). The path is clockwise around the board.

2. Starting the Game

  • At the start, all 4 tokens for each player are placed in their respective "Yard" or Base.
  • To enter a token into active play, you must roll a SIX (6) on the dice.
  • If you do not roll a 6, the turn passes to the next player.

3. Movement & The "Six" Rule

Once a token is in play, you move it forward by the number of squares indicated by the dice roll.

  • Bonus Turn: Rolling a 6 gives you an extra roll. You can use the 6 to move a piece or bring a new piece out, and then roll again.
  • Consecutive Sixes: If you roll 6 three times in a row, your turn is forfeited immediately to prevent unfair advantages.

4. Capturing Opponents (Cutting)

This is where the strategy begins. If your token lands on a square currently occupied by an opponent's token:

  • The opponent's token is captured and sent back to their Base.
  • The opponent must roll a 6 again to restart that token.
  • Note: You cannot capture a token if it is on a "Safe Zone" (marked with a Star or Globe).

5. Safe Zones

There are specific squares on the board marked with a Star icon. These are Safe Zones. No player can be captured while their token rests on these squares. It is a strategic advantage to keep your tokens here while waiting for a good roll.

6. Reaching Home (Winning)

When a token completes the full track, it enters the colored "Home Column."

  • You must roll the exact number needed to reach the center Home triangle.
  • For example, if you are 3 squares away, you must roll a 3 (or a 1 and then a 2).
  • The first player to get all 4 tokens into the Home triangle wins the game!

Pro Tips for Strategy

To maximize your winning chances on Ludo MINIMA:

  • Open All Tokens: Don't just rely on one token. Try to get all 4 out of the base as soon as possible.
  • Camp on Stars: Use safe zones to hide from opponents chasing you.
  • Calculate Risk: Before making a move, check if you are landing 1-6 steps ahead of an opponent who might cut you on their next turn.
